Services Offers

The HRD or Human Resource Department is an important part of the organization.
HRD is a division that was created to manage human resources, starting from gathering manpower to developing it to become human resource that can move in a structured manner and achieve organizational goals.

Recruitment and Selection

This task is very influential on the next HRD task. Choosing human resources who are able to work with us is not easy. If you fail to get potential Human Resources, HRD will face difficulties in developing talent, and high turnover ratio.

Training dan Development

HRD must fully support education and development programs for employees. Companies need people who can produce good results in a fast-changing environment in order for the company to survive.

Compensation and Benefit

HRD has the authority to design compensation and benefit programs. Set the scheme and distribution procedures. Because this issue is quite sensitive and concerns the welfare of employees.

Personnel Administration

Responsible for employee database, payroll, attendance management, annual leave recording, and work contract system. Personnel management can help reduce labor costs, motivate employees, and increase profits.

Performance Appraisal

Evaluation is a moment where the company assesses employee performance as well as reflects on the training and development programs provided to employees. Evaluation is very important and plays a role in the growth of the company.

Career Planning

HRD must be able to design career planning programs for each layer of employees, so that employees who work in the company have the motivation to achieve these targets and are not easy to think about moving to another company.

Creating a Good Work Environment

Ensuring Employees Don't Violate Company Regulations
Committed to Company Value
Bridging the Employee-Management Layer Communication

Designing Useful Programs in the Company

Designing beneficial programs for employees is one of the efforts made by HRD to help employees better understand the company where they work. The company hopes that all levels of employees have the same vision and mission, and also strong sense of belonging.
